Model Training and Testing
--------------------------

Model training is listed from oldest to newest (newest at the bottom).

After model testing, it is useful to rank the model analyses:

    # Ranking of the fixed grid dataset
    python3 main.py rank_analysis_dir fixed_50nm_range_processed \
        --ds-on-fixed-grid --r-min-filter 0.4

    # Or, ranking of another testing dataset, for instance `test_ran50nm_gl_lg_diff`
    python3 main.py rank_analysis_dir test_ran50nm_gl_lg_diff

[V1] The `ran50nm_gl_lg_diff_` tag:

    python3 main_stnp.py batch_model_train \
        train_ran50nm_gl_lg_diff val_ran50nm_gl_lg_diff \
        ran50nm_gl_lg_diff_ 500 --max-threads 4 \
        --networks dfc1 dfc2 sc1 btbc1 btbc2 \
        --losses mse --optimizers adam \
        --lrs 1e-3 5e-4 1e-4 --batch-sizes 64 128 \
        --overwrite-existing --only-best-epoch --early-stopping 10

    python3 main.py batch_model_test \
